Lanterns

Within the DC Universe, there are numerous Lantern Corps. One for each color in the color spectrum. As such, there are also a lot of stories and and characters that go into it. This page will contain information on comics from the Lanterns.

Lantern Entities


Adara: the entity for the Blue Lantern Corps that draws their power from hope.
The Butcher: the entity for the Red Lanterns that draws their power from rage.
Ion: the entity for the Green Lantern Corps that draws their power from willpower.
Ophidian: the entity for the Orange Lantern Crops that draws their power from avarice.
Parallax: the entity for the Sinestro Corps that draws their power from fear.
Predator: the entity for the Star Sapphires that draws their power from love.
Proselyte: the entity for the Indigo Tribe that draws their power from compassion.
The Entity: the entity for the White Lanterns. The Entity provides the spark in the universe that gives life to all beings.
Nekron: the leader and entity of the Black Lanterns that seek to suck enotion and life out of the universe to make it an empty void.
